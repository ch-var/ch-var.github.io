Our article featured amongst the most-read recent articles in Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society

According to Oxford University Press

Posted on May 2, 2025

I am excited to share that our research article “SMART: Spectral Energy Distributions Markov Chain Analysis with Radiative Transfer Models” is amongst the most-read recent articles in the Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society, according to Oxford University Press.

I am grateful for the interest in our work and thrilled to see the SMART methodology gaining recognition in the astrophysical community. The article is available at https://doi.org/10.1093/mnras/stae1141. The code is available at https://github.com/ch-var/SMART.
