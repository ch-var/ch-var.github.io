Software Development

SMART: Spectral energy distributions Markov chain Analysis with Radiative Transfer models

An open-source tool that implements a Bayesian Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) method to fit the ultraviolet to millimetre spectral energy distributions (SEDs) of galaxies exclusively with radiative transfer models
