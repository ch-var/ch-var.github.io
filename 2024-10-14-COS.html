“Exploring the properties of the obscured quasar COS-87259 at \(z\)=6.853”

Published in Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society

『✎』Posted on April 30, 2024

I am delighted to announce the publication of our research article in Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society, entitled “Exploring the properties of the obscured quasar COS-87259 at \(z\)=6.853”.

In this article we explore the properties of the obscured hyperluminous quasar COS-87259, discovered in the Cosmological Evolution Survey (COSMOS) field, with our recently developed Bayesian spectral energy distribution (SED) fitting code SMART (Spectral energy distributions Markov chain Analysis with Radiative Transfer models).

For this work we acknowledge support from the projects CYGNUS and CYGNUS+ funded by the European Space Agency.
